Grainger Engineering Webinars

About

Grainger Engineering Webinars are hosted three times each semester. Our live webinars give prospective engineers the opportunity to hear about engineering majors, academics, applications, and campus life. Additionally, at the end of each session you will get the opportunity to ask current students and an engineering dean any questions you may have.

For any questions related to Grainger Engineering Webinars, please contact Alp Aktuna at aaktuna2@illinois.edu

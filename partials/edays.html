Grainger Engineering Days

About

Grainger Engineering Days are one of the best ways for prospective engineering students to see what being an engineer is all about. The University of Illinois and Engineering Council would like to invite you to attend the next program!

As a high school student, you have probably begun to consider different majors of study and which school to attend after graduation. Grainger Engineering Days can begin to help the questions that you have as you start thinking about a career in engineering.

We are very excited to bring back in-person E-Days this year while continuing our virtual seminars to accomodate those who cannot travel to visit us on campus. You will learn about the different engineering fields, talk to current college students about life as an engineering student, and get a good look at one of the nation's top rated engineering universities.

About Us

The Engineering Outreach Bureau is one of 15 committees in Engineering Council. Our programs are developed and implemented entirely by current engineering students and are designed to inform interested individuals and groups, most often prospective students and their parents.

Our Programs

 

Grainger Engineering Days

Open to anyone, Grainger Engineering Days are intended to introduce high school students to the many engineering curriculums offered at the University of Illinois. Students will learn about different engineering majors, chat with engineering students, and much more.

Shadow an Engineer

Shadow an Engineer allows admitted students to spend a day with a current U of I engineering student and experience campus life firsthand. Shadowing students will sit in on lectures, and most importantly, get time to talk to current students about their experiences. NOTE: this program is for admitted students only - parents/siblings will not accompany the shadowing student.

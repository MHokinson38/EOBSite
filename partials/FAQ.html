Why is there a cost?

The $20 fee per student accounts for lunch and a bag of materials they receive on E-Day. The $10 per additional guest accounts for lunch. There is an option to prepay for a parking pass  in the parking deck for $20. Since this is a completely student run event, we have to raise money for every part of the event.

Where will we be eating lunch?

Lunch is catered from a restaurant or catering company. If you have allergies or dietary restrictions, please indicate them in the registration form so we can account for them!

Can we get a tour of a residence hall?

While EOB does not facilitate residence hall tours, they may be available during your visit: http://housing.illinois.edu/aboutus/hall-tours

When will we receive a schedule and instructions for the day?

You will receive multiple confirmations for E-Day in the weeks leading up to the event. A full itinerary including check-in and parking instructions will be sent out by email no later than 10 days before your visit. A sample schedule can be found on the EOB website.

I am interested in a major that is not one of the options for tours, how do I get a tour for that major?

There are certain majors that we do not offer tours for due to minimal interest of that the facilities and labs are currently being renovated. If there is a specific major you are interested in, contact the committee in advance and we can try to set up a personal tour for you during that time.

I already paid online, can I get a refund if I can no longer attend?

If you cancel your reservation at least one week before the event, a full refund can be processed.

Is there parking available for the day?

You have the option to purchase a parking pass for the day during registration. We will have spots available for you at the parking deck close to the event. Otherwise, meter parking is an option.
 

We are traveling from out of state and will have to come to campus the night before. Does EOB or the University offer housing?

Unfortunately, EOB or the university cannot house students or their families the night before. There are many hotels on and very near campus for great prices. If you would like some recommendations, please contact the EOB Director.
